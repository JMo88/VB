Why VoltaBase?

VoltaBase is autonomous base that uses hot-swapping technology to allow drones to land, replace their batteries within minutes, and continue the mission without missing a beat.

Complete Autonomy

VoltaBase communicates directly with autonomous drones, allowing them to replace dead batteries and cargo with zero manual intervention.

Hot-Swapping

Patented Hot-Swapping technology allows the drone to continue running, even while the battery is being replaced.

Increased Flight Range

Installing one VoltaBase triples maximum flight distance and doubles total flight area for drones.

Customization

VoltaBase can be used with a variety of drone types and purposes. From surveillance drones, to pesticide drones, the options are unlimited.
